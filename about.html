How does it work?

Tagify connects with your spotify library and helps you find the perfect music for your mood. All without playlists.


It's as easy as 1, 2, 3

  1. Download the Tagify App
  2. Tagify your library by assigning tags to your songs
  3. Filter your songs by the tags you want to listen to
